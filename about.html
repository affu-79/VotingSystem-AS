About VotingSystem AS

Empowering fair and transparent elections through blockchain technology. Built by dreamers, secured by code.

Our Mission

To revolutionize digital voting systems by providing a secure, decentralized, and tamper-proof platform accessible to every citizen.

Vision

To establish global trust in online voting systems using the power of decentralized ledger technology.

Integrity

We believe trust is earned through transparency, honesty, and continuous improvement.

Innovation

We push boundaries with smart contracts, AI, and scalable blockchain integrations.

Our Journey

2023 - Conceptualization

The idea to combat election fraud and increase voter trust was born.

2024 - Prototype Launch

A proof-of-concept was launched using Solidity smart contracts on the Sepolia testnet.

2025 - Full Platform Build

With feedback and technical upgrades, the full version was deployed with MySQL-Python backend and advanced UI.
